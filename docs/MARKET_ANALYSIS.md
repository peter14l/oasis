# Oasis: Market Opportunity & Business Case (India 2024-2030)

**Executive Summary:** Oasis is uniquely positioned to capture the intersection of three high-growth sectors in India: **Digital Wellbeing**, **Private Communications**, and **Subscription-based Social Media**. As India moves toward a "post-dopamine" digital economy, Oasis provides the infrastructure for intentional relationships.

---

## 📈 Market Size (TAM / SAM / SOM)

### 1. TAM (Total Addressable Market): $3.5 Billion
*   **Segment:** The combined Indian Digital Health and Wellness App market.
*   **Driver:** India’s 900M+ smartphone users (by 2030) increasingly spending on "preventive" digital tools.
*   **Growth:** The sector is growing at a **16.2% CAGR**.

### 2. SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): $1.1 Billion
*   **Segment:** Urban Gen-Z and Millennial professionals (Tier 1 & 2 cities) who show signs of "digital burnout" and are currently paying for at least one premium digital subscription (YouTube Premium, Meta Verified, or Headspace/Wysa).
*   **Stat:** **37% of Indian adolescents** and **36.9% of college students** show signs of problematic social media use, creating a massive demand for "Intentional" alternatives.

### 3. SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): $45 Million
*   **Target:** 1 million "Pro" subscribers within 3 years (representing ~0.1% of the Indian smartphone base).
*   **Revenue Model:** Oasis "Covenant" Subscription at ₹149/month (~$1.80) or ₹999/year.
*   **Status:** **Razorpay production-ready** for immediate Indian market rollout; RevenueCat backbone prepped for future global IAP scaling.

## 🇮🇳 Why India? Why Now?

1.  **The DPDP Act (2023) Catalyst:** India's new data protection law requires "verifiable consent" and strictly prohibits tracking minors. Oasis's **Privacy-First architecture** is natively compliant, while legacy platforms are struggling to retroactively fix their data models.
2.  **The "Premiumization" Trend:** 2024 marked a shift where Indians lead the world in **Meta AI** adoption and are rapidly adopting **YouTube Premium**. The "willingness to pay" for better experiences is at an all-time high.
3.  **Digital Health Sovereignty:** The Indian government's **Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM)** has primed the population to value their own digital health records and sovereignty—a core pillar of Oasis.

## 🚀 The Growth Narrative for Investors

Oasis is not competing for "time spent"; we are competing for **"Relational Quality."** 

By removing the infinite feed and replacing it with **Relational Circles** and **Ambient Wellness UI**, we solve the problem that 73% of Indians report: *feeling that their data and attention are being gathered without their knowledge.*

**Our "Unfair Advantage":**
- **Surgical Pivot:** We have already built the "complex" tech (WebRTC Calls, E2EE, Subscriptions) and are now surgically removing the "toxic" features (Feed, Metrics).
- **Hybrid Security:** We use Signal-grade signaling with RSA/AES E2EE, giving us a technical defensibility moat over simple "chat" wrappers.
