# Morrow V4.0 Update Changelog 🚀

Welcome to Morrow V4.0. This update marks our transition from a functional utility to a premium, kinetic, and deeply immersive social engine. We have completely overhauled the messaging experience, enhanced privacy controls, and redesigned our digital presence for the upcoming private beta.

---

## 1. "Ethereal" Inbox Overhaul
The conversation list has been transformed into a physics-based, living environment.

- **Kinetic Scrolling:** Implemented "Squish & Stretch" physics. List items now dynamically compress and expand based on scroll velocity, providing an organic, tactile feel.
- **Bento-Grid Favorites:** Pinned chats now live in a non-linear "Bento Box" at the top of the inbox. 
    - **Freely Resizable:** Users can **Long-Press** any favorite card to cycle it through three distinct sizes (Compact, Tall, Wide).
    - **Size Persistence:** Custom layouts are now saved to the device and persist across restarts.
- **Presence Ripples:** Traditional online dots are replaced with soft blue breathing rings around avatars.
- **Typing Auras:** A glowing, vibe-matched radial gradient now ripples behind any contact that is currently typing.
- **Vibe-Based Backgrounds:** Every chat tile now features a unique glassmorphic background tinted with a color derived from the user's name.

---

## 2. "Stealth Preview" System
A new way to stay connected without the pressure of immediate responses.

- **Pop-up Peeking:** Long-press any "Recent" bubble to open a high-sigma blurred glassmorphic preview window.
- **Multi-Message Support:** The preview window is now taller and scrollable, allowing you to read the entire unread history of a chat.
- **Zero Read-Receipts:** Peeking at messages through the Stealth Preview **will not** trigger a "Read" status or send notifications to the other user.
- **Tactile Feedback:** Integrated heavy-impact haptics when the preview activates for a premium iOS-like feel.

---

## 3. Privacy & Safety Engine (Pro)
Enhanced controls to give you absolute power over your digital space.

- **Global Mute Logic:**
    - New "Mute Notifications" toggle in Chat Details.
    - **Total Silence:** Muted chats are suppressed at the database level—no push notifications, no in-app banners, and no system alerts.
- **Robust Blocking:**
    - Implemented a "Proper Block" system that prevents blocked users from sending messages entirely.
    - Blocking is enforced at the database level using Row Level Security (RLS) and verified by backend Edge Functions.
- **Persistence:** All mute and block settings are stored in the Supabase cloud and persist across app reinstalls.

---

## 4. Performance & Visual Modes
Tailoring the experience for speed and battery life.

- **Dark Opaque Mode (Pro):**
    - Introduced a deep, OLED-black opaque background (`0xFF080A0E`).
    - **Battery Optimization:** Significant reduction in GPU overhead compared to mesh gradients.
    - **High-Contrast:** Makes kinetic animations and typing auras "pop" with extreme clarity.
- **Mesh Background Toggle:** Users can now toggle between the "Living Mesh" and "Pro Opaque" modes in Settings.
- **Battery Warning:** Added clear documentation in-app about the power trade-offs of the dynamic mesh background.

---

## 5. Official Landing Page Redesign
Our web presence has been rebuilt from the ground up for the private beta.

- **Modern Aesthetic:** "Apple-like" kinetic scroll animations and a vibrant Gen Z color palette.
- **Beta Slot Tracker:** Real-time counter showing `X / 20 SLOTS TAKEN`, linked directly to the live database.
- **APK Lock:** The download button automatically disables and greys out once the 20-tester limit is reached.
- **PayU Integration:** 
    - Full implementation of the **PayU India** payment gateway.
    - Secure backend SHA-512 hash generation via Supabase Edge Functions.
    - Localized pricing for USD, INR, EUR, and GBP (Purchasing Power Parity).

---

## 6. Technical Cleanup & Optimizations
- **Version Bump:** Upgraded system version to **4.0.0+1**.
- **APK Size Reduction:** Completely removed FFmpeg and all audio compression logic, reducing the binary size by **~40MB**.
- **Dependency Resolution:** Fixed Android duplicate class errors (`androidx.annotation`) for a cleaner, faster build process.
- **Bug Fixes:** Resolved multiple RenderFlex overflows in the Bento grid and fixed end-to-end decryption logic in search modals.
